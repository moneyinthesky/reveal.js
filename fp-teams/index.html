In reality, potential employers may be interested in your project, but they'll want to ask you some follow up questions...

Technical Questions

  • Why did you choose the languages (JavaScript, Python, Typescript, etc.) and frameworks (Express, React, etc.) that you did?
  • What technical challenges did you run into / how did you overcome them?
  • How did you work with Git as a team (PR's, code reviews, merge conflicts, etc.)?
  • ...and more...

Interpersonal Questions

  • How did you communicate?
  • How did you balance your ideas with the ideas of others?
  • Did you run into any conflicts? How did you handle them?
  • ...and many more...

Agile Questions

  • How did you plan your work?
  • How did you break down and estimate your tasks?
  • How did you structure your stand-up and other meetings?
  • How did you ensure 2 people were not working on the same thing?
  • Can you talk about any feedback loops? (1) feedback about the product from your "user" and (2) feedback about how the team is working from the members of the team

For our goal
